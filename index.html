About Us

Time4Recruitment

We are an independent, privately owned recruitment agency that matches the right people to the right contract opportunities in the Construction, Mechanical & Electrical, Industrial and Catering and Hospitality sectors throughout the UK. A family-feel company, we offer flexible and supportive careers to our employees and as such have a very high staff retention and referral rate meaning you get to develop long-standing relationships with our team. We offer a personal, professional and quality service to all our clients and workers – listening to and caring about your needs; as captured in our strap line ‘the agency that cares’.

Client Services

Construction

We find work for, and supply, construction workers for projects from small fit out contracts to very large multi-million pound new build schemes including building, infrastructure, soft and hard facilities management, interiors and engineering construction.

Mechanical & Electrical

We supply mechanical, electrical or engineering construction workers throughout the UK on a temporary, freelance or permanent basis including in the power industry or projects requiring high security clearance.

Industrial

We can provide industrial operatives anywhere in the UK for commercial cleaning, warehousing, manufacturing and production, facilities management, waste management, transport, logistics and distribution.

Catering

Time 4 Recruitment is experienced in supplying all levels of catering and hospitality positions for front of house, kitchen, banqueting services and events, to cover busy periods or on a permanent basis.

Why choose us?

As a friendly, caring and professional agency we: will help you register with us in the easiest way possible for you from one of our main offices in Cardiff, Bristol or London. supply over 350 valued clients throughout the UK and are on the Preferred Supplier Lists for a significant number of key, national companies, so will match you to the right contract or position. can help you find a temporary, freelance contract or permanent position. always aim to keep our quality workers in work, key selling your skills to our clients, keeping in contact to find out your availability and letting you know vacancy opportunities. can help you with CV and interview skills if you are looking for a freelance contract or permanent position. have our own in-house payroll team to make timesheet collection easy and ensure correct and prompt payment. listen to you, our candidates, to find out what you require from a recruitment agency rather than just seeing you as a number.
